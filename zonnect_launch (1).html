1. Who we are

Zonnect connects you with a trusted local agent in your chosen city who carries out errands, sourcing, shopping, and on-ground tasks on your behalf, billed at ₹49 per hour.

2. Booking & service time

Time is billed from when the agent starts your task to when it's completed, rounded to the nearest 15 minutes. Estimated arrival is within 1 hour of order confirmation, depending on agent availability in your city.

3. Pricing

All services are billed at ₹49 per hour, rounded to the nearest 15 minutes, starting from when the agent begins your task.

4. Shipping & delivery

  • For Shopping & Sourcing orders, standard local delivery charges apply and are added to your final bill.
  • Free shipping is included on any Shopping & Sourcing order where the total agent service time exceeds 4 hours, provided the total item weight is under 1 kg.
  • Delivery timelines depend on the item, agent location, and city traffic — these are estimates, not guarantees.

5. Weight limit for shopping & sourcing

  • Items purchased and delivered through Zonnect's Shopping & Sourcing service must weigh less than 1 kg in total, per order.
  • Orders involving heavier, bulky, or fragile items beyond this limit may be declined, split into multiple bookings, or require a separate courier arrangement at your cost.
  • You must confirm the 1 kg limit at the time of booking. Misrepresenting item weight may result in additional charges or order cancellation.

6. Returns & replacement

  • If an item purchased on your behalf is damaged, defective, or doesn't match what was agreed before purchase, contact us within 48 hours of delivery for a return or replacement.
  • Zonnect will coordinate the return/replacement with the original seller where possible; outcomes depend on that seller's own return policy, since Zonnect is acting as your purchasing agent, not the seller.
  • Perishable goods, customized/made-to-order items, and items explicitly purchased "as-is" at your instruction are not eligible for return.
  • Agent service fees already billed (time spent sourcing/purchasing) are non-refundable even if the item itself is returned, unless the error was caused by the agent.

7. Payments

Payment is collected through our secure payment gateway. All charges (service hours, applicable shipping, and item cost where Zonnect has advanced payment on your behalf) are shown before you confirm.

8. Cancellations

You may cancel a request before an agent is assigned at no cost. Once an agent has started work, cancellation charges may apply for time already spent.

9. Your responsibilities

You agree to provide accurate details (item description, quantity, weight, budget, delivery address) so the agent can complete your request correctly. Zonnect is not liable for delays or errors caused by incomplete or incorrect information you provided.

10. Limitation of liability

Zonnect and its agents act in good faith on your instructions. We are not liable for losses beyond the value of the specific order, except in cases of proven negligence or misconduct by an agent.

11. Changes to these terms

We may update these terms from time to time. Continued use of Zonnect after an update means you accept the revised terms.

12. Contact us

📦 Return & Replacement Policy

Eligibility

Returns and replacements are accepted if the item received is damaged, defective, or significantly different from what was agreed before purchase. You must raise a request within 48 hours of delivery by contacting us on WhatsApp or email.

How to raise a return

  • Message us on WhatsApp +91 92292 18181 with your Order ID and photos of the item.
  • We will coordinate with the seller on your behalf.
  • If the seller approves the return, your item will be picked up and a replacement or refund will be processed within 5–7 business days.

Non-returnable items

  • Perishable goods (food, flowers, etc.)
  • Customised or made-to-order items
  • Items purchased "as-is" at your explicit instruction
  • Items damaged after delivery due to customer handling

Agent fee refund

Agent service fees (time billed) are non-refundable once work has begun, unless the error was caused by the agent (e.g. wrong item purchased despite correct instructions).
